Philosophy

How I see
the world

I am an African Renaissance artist rooted in Brooklyn, with ancestral ties to Ivory Coast and Nigeria. My work lives at the intersection of spatial art, pan-African philosophy, and the technologies of memory — quantum, ancestral, and ecological.

I believe that art is not decoration. It is a technology. A ceremony. A way of transmitting what cannot be said in plain language.
